Typical 15-Day Itinerary

Days 1 - 3: Arrival in Madinah

Arrival at Prince Mohammad bin Abdulaziz Airport. Check-in and start of Salutations to the Prophet (PBUH). Experience the peace of the Masjid an-Nabawi.

Days 4 - 6: Madinah Ziyarat

Guided visits to Mount Uhud, Quba Mosque, and Masjid al-Qiblatayn. Spiritual preparation for Makkah.

Day 7: The Journey to Makkah

Putting on Ihram at Miqat and traveling via High-Speed Rail or Luxury Coach. Performance of the first Umrah.

Days 8 - 14: Stay in Makkah

Daily prayers in Masjid al-Haram. Optional guided Ziyarat to Cave of Hira, Cave of Thawr, and Arafat.

Day 15: Farewell & Return

Farewell Tawaf and transfer to Jeddah King Abdulaziz Airport for departure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries regarding your spiritual travel.

How long does the Umrah visa process take? expand_more
Typically, the electronic Umrah visa process takes between 24 to 72 hours. However, we recommend applying at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure all logistics are coordinated.
Are vaccinations mandatory for entry? expand_more
Yes, the Saudi Ministry of Health requires proof of Meningococcal (ACWY) vaccination. Depending on your country of origin, other vaccinations like Polio or Yellow Fever may be required.
How close are the hotels to the Haram? expand_more
Our VIP packages feature hotels directly on the Haram courtyard. Standard packages are within a 5-10 minute walk (approx. 300m), and Economy packages provide 24/7 shuttle services for hotels further away.
